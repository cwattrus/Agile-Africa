Conference Venue

Focus Rooms is a unique conference venue set in the heart of Sunninghill. The area is a hub of business in the northern suburbs of Johannesburg, and even if your business isn't in close proximity, it's easily accessible.

Accomodation:

The organizers would happily arrange accommodation at the facilities where the Master classes and the main conference is being held for delegates. If you would however prefer to stay elsewhere, we'd be happy to assist with that too! The Master classes and the Conference is in close proximity to Gautrain stations which connect to JHB International Airport. If you would like to journey around the Republic and need advice, please do let us know if you require assistance.
